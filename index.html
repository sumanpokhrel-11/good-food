chow Mein

Chow mein is a Chinese dish made from stir-fried noodles with vegetables and sometimes meat or tofu. there are several methods of frying the noodles and a range of toppings can be used. The dish is popular throughout the Chinese diaspora and appears on the menus of most Chinese restaurants abroad. It is particularly popular in India, Nepal, the UK, and the US.

Nepali culture is rich in food. Nepali khana and Nepali parikar are often described as being spicy but are appreciated for their authentic taste and, of course, their mouth-watering delicious taste.
